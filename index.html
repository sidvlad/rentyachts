Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about yacht rental

For yacht rental you need a valid passport or ID. If you plan to captain the yacht yourself, you'll need a valid boating license. For chartered trips with our captain, no license is required.

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (June-September). However, last-minute bookings are possible subject to availability.

Fuel costs are typically included for standard routes. For longer trips or custom routes, fuel may be charged separately. Please check the specific yacht listing or ask our team for details.

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the trip. Cancellations within 48 hours are subject to a 50% fee. In case of bad weather, we offer full rescheduling or refund.
